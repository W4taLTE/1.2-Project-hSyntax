üí° Struktur Fundamental

Setiap dokumen HTML memerlukan struktur dasar yang terdiri dari deklarasi DOCTYPE, elemen HTML, HEAD, dan BODY.

index.html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
  <meta charset="UTF-8">
  <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
  <title>Document</title>
</head>
<body>
  <!-- Your amazing content goes here -->
  <h1>Welcome to HTML!</h1>
  <p>This is your first paragraph.</p>
</body>
</html>
